Frequently Asked Questions

You ask, and we answer.

Having a website built can be an overwhelming experience, even for the most IT savvy people. At Mity Digital, we are here to support you through the whole process to help create a website that will deliver results for your business.

  1. How well does MyPortfolio help you?

    This website allows you to find and associate good future options for you! Be it finding you a job, referring you to other employers.

  2. How long does does the course take?

    The time it takes to produce a website depends on the size and complexity of your site. A basic website typically takes around 4-6 weeks from beginning to end. Some more custom and complex project may take longer.

  3. Will my website work on mobile devices?

    Yes, we ensure our sites work on all devices, including desktops, laptops, tablets and mobiles.

  4. Do you build mobile apps?

    No, we don't - but we do make our websites work on all devices. Developing an app for mobiles and tablets across the range of different platforms can be an expensive task, and in some cases can be resolved with a responsive website.

  5. Can I sell products on my website?

    Sure thing - our content management system can build and host your online store, and we can set up all of the security necessary to ensure your customers' details remain safe and secure.

  6. What is a Content Management System (CMS)?

    A content management system (CMS) enables you to maintain the content on your website without any additional software our technical skill. When using a CMS, you can add new pages, upload images and be in full control of your site - all you need is a web browser and an internet connection.
